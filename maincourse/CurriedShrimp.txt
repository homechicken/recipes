Alaskan Curried Shrimp

1 lb medium to small prawns
1 medium onion
3 T butter
2 T flour
2 T curry powder
1 t salt
1 1/2 c milk with 1/2 c water

Boil and shuck prawns when they float they're done!

Saut√© the onion in the butter. Over medium heat, add the flour, curry powder, and salt, stirring frequently.

When it makes a pasty product, remove to double boiler and add the milk/water mixture slowly, stirring frequently to thicken up your creation. When it reaches a nice warm temperature add shrimp. Remove from heat and let stand a few minutes.

Makes 3 really healthy servings.
