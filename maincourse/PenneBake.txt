Penne Bake

3 c dried pasta
1 c fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 c onion, chopped onion
2-3 medium green or red sweet peppers cut in strips
6oz sliced pepperoni or canadian bacon cut up
1 T minced garlic
1T Olive or cooking oil
1 26-29oz ( 3 Cups) marinara pasta sauce
1/4c (5oz) shredded italian blend cheese

Set oven to 350F

Lightly coat a baking dish with cooking spray and set aside. Cook pasta according to package directions and drain well. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook mushrooms, onion, peppers, pepperoni, and garlic in hot oil for 3 minutes. Add vegetable mix, pasta, and sauce together and toss to coat. Spread mixture evenly in prepared baking dish. (Freeze now if not eating right away)

Bake, covered with foil, at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Uncover and sprinkle with cheese and bake until cheese melts, about 5 minutes.
