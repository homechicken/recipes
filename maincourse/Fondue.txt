Fondue

1/3 pound gruyere
1/3 pound gouda
1/3 pound fontina
2 T cornstarch
1 c dry white wine such as Sauvignon Blanc
1 clove garlic, minced
1 T fresh lemon juice
1 T brandy or Kirsch
1/8 t nutmeg (optional)

Grate all cheeses, toss in bowl with cornstarch until thoroughly coated.

Bring lemon juice, wine, and garlic to simmer over medium-low heat. Slowly add cheese a little at a time, stirring well between each addition to ensure a smooth fondue. Once melted and smooth, add the final ingredients and stir.

Dipping recommendations:
boiled baby potatoes
broccoli or cauliflower florets
button mushrooms
cherry tomatoes
sliced firm apples such as grannysmith
sliced sausage
cubed french, sourdough, or pumpernickel bread
