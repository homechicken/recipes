Crispy Pork Belly

Recipe:     Prep Time:   15-20mins          Cook Time: 2 Hours +
                  Cooking Temperature:  350 Degrees F
Ingredients:
Pork Belly - 1-2 Lb Strips
Salt - 1-2 Table Spoons 
Water - Enough to Cover Roast 3/4 of the Way in your chosen pan
Suggested Seasonings - Garlic, Italian Seasoning, Salt & Pepper.

Step 1: Cover Pork Belly in salt, Scrub for 1 minute bu hand. Rinse well.
Step 2: Boil Pork Belly in water covered 3/4 of the way up. Boil for 10 - 15 minutes, or until roast turns a grayish color.
Step 3: Cut Grooves in both Directions 1/4 inch apart: Then lightly salt. 
Step 4: Bake in your preheated oven at 350 degrees F for 2 hours 
Step 5: Take out of oven and Use a Brush or spoon to take rendered fat from the pan below and coat the top of the Pork Belly. Then you may Season as you like and Place it bake in the oven for another 10 Minutes, Paying attention as to not burn the Seasonings.
Rest for a few minutes and Enjoy in your favorite pork Belly Recipe or just as is!
