Scottish Oatcakes

30 minutes, makes 12-16 cakes

2 cups medium oatmeal plust extra for dusting
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
4 T butter or lard
4-6 T warm water

Mix the dry ingredients, stir in melted butter (or lard) until crumbly. Gradually add warm water until a firm but pliable dough forms.

On a floured surface (use the extra oatmeal for this), roll dough to 1/4" thickness, cut into rounds or traditional Scottish triangular "farls".

Place on a hot griddle or frying pan and cook for 3-4 minutes per side until golden and crisp. Alternatively, bake in a hot oven at 400F for 15-20 minutes.

Best enjoyed when warm.
