Whole Wheat Bread
SUMMARY:

A great, 100% whole-wheat bread!

INGREDIENTS:

  Step 1
- 4 teaspoons yeast
- 3 1/2 cups white whole wheat flour
- 1/3 cup wheat gluten
- 2 1/2 cups warm water
  Step 2
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 4 teaspoons orange juice
- 2 1/2 cups white whole wheat flour


METHOD:

1. Mix together all dry ingredients from Step 1. Add water and mix until incorporated, scraping bowl as needed. Cover and let rest 10 minutes.

2. Add salt, oil, honey, and lemon juice. Mix until combined. Add remaining flour slowly as it is incorporated. Knead until smooth, about 5 minutes in a mixer.

3. Let rise until doubled. Punch down, separate dough into two pieces, and press into loaf pans. Proof until tops are puffing up over the edge of the loaf pans. Preheat oven to 350. Bake bread for 30 minutes, rotating the pans once. When finished, remove the loaves immediately and place on a cooling rack.
