Overnight Oats

Basic recipe
1/2 c rolled oats (do not use instant)
pinch of salt

More flavors
cocoa powder
cinnamon
vanilla
fruit drink powder

Extra add-ins
chia seeds
ground flax meal
hemp hearts
chocolate sprinkles
shredded coconut

Sweeteners
sugar
brown sugar
honey
maple syrup


Directions
Pour 8 oz of milk into a shaker glass or canning jar
Add oats and salt, plus any additional mix-ins and sweeteners you want
Shake well. Refrigerate at least a few hours, overnight is ideal.
Drink cold, or microwave for 1 minute for a hot drink.
