Barbecue Rub
SUMMARY:

Double this and it fits easily into a quart-sized canning jar. Store with an airtight lid out of sunlight and it will last at least six months.

INGREDIENTS:

- 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
- 1/4 cup sweet paprika
- 3 tablespoons black pepper
- 4 tablespoons coarse salt
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- 2 teaspoons celery seed
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper


METHOD:

Mix all ingredients well. Break up any lumps of brown sugar. Hands may work better than a spoon or whisk.
