Five Spice Powder

2 t Sichuan peppercorns (use black peppercorns for no heat)
5-6 whole star anise
1/2 t ground cloves
1 T ground cinnamon
1 T ground fennel

Toast the peppercorns in a dry skillet over low to medium heat until you smell them, about 3 minutes.

Grind the peppercorns and star anise until fine, pass this through a fine mest strainer for even consistency.

Mix in other spices, grind until very fine.
