Berbere
An Ethiopian spice blend, use as a spice rub or dusting for stews and lentils. A little goes a long way.

1 T smoked paprika
1 t each of ground coriander, ground ginger
1/2 t cayenne pepper
1/4 t ground cardamom

Toast dry spices in a skillet over medium-low heat for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.

Stir in 1/2 t kosher salt off heat.
