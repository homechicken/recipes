Jerk-Style Spice Rub

INGREDIENTS
1	tablespoon allspice berries
1	tablespoon black peppercorns
1 ½	teaspoons dried thyme
2	tablespoons packed brown sugar
2	teaspoons garlic powder
¾	teaspoon salt
¾	teaspoon cayenne pepper

If you can't find allspice berries, substitute 2 tsp of ground allspice

Instructions

Grind allspice, peppercorns, and thyme in spice grinder or mortar and pestle until coarsely ground. Transfer to bowl and stir in sugar, garlic powder, salt, and cayenne.
