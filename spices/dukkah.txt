Dukkah
An Egyptian spice blend for sprinkling on flatbread, hard-cooked eggs, roasted vegetables, and meats. Sprinkle over soups, stews, and salads or mix with olive oil for dipping.

1/2 c cashews
2 T sesame seeds
2 T each of coriander, cumin seeds
1 T caraway seeds
1 t dried oregano
1/2 t each of kosher salt, black pepper

Toast cashews over medium heat for 3-4 minutes, add sesame seeds.
Toast for 1-2 minutes, then add coriander, cumin, and caraway.
Toast for 1 minute.
Pulse in food processor, add oregano, salt, and pepper.
