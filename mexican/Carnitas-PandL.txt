Carnitas

1 pork butt roast, cut into chunks 
1 c water
4 T white pepper
2 bay leaves
1/4 c orange juice
1/4 c Coke
3 T salt
vegetable oil

Cover the meat chunks with vegetable oil. Add the water and bring to a boil. Cook pork until almost done. Combine the orange juice and Coke in a glass and add. Finish cooking until pork is golden brown. Add salt.

From Pancho & Lefty's Restaurant cookbook
