Green Chili Chorizo Burgers

1 4-oz can chopped green chilis, drained
3 T salsa verde
2 T finely choppen onion
1 pound ground pork
1 pound Chorizo sausage, casings removed
1 t salt
1 t pepper
4 oz Monterey Jack cheese
4 buns, toasted
Lettuce, etc fixings

Combine chiles, salsa verde, and onion in small bowl, set aside.

Combine pork and chorizo, form into 8 equal balls and press into 5-inch patties. Sprinkle with salt and peper

Grill patties until well browned. Flip and top with 1 T chile mixture and 2 T cheese. Continue to grill until second side is done.
