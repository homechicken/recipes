Pancho & Lefty's Fresh Salsa

2 chopped onions, Maui or sweet
4 tomatoes, chopped
1/2 bunch cilantro or less, to taste
1-2 serrano chiles, seeds removed and chopped fine. Omit for mild salsa

Mix in blender:
3 garlic cloves
1 T white pepper
1 T Mexican oregano
1 can whole tomatoes

Add to salsa. Add salt to taste and maybe a little granulated garlic.

From Pancho & Lefty's Restaurant cookbook
