Mexican Rice

INGREDIENTS:

3 tablespoons vegetable oil
1 cup long grain white rice, uncooked
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 cup tomato sauce
2 cups chicken broth


METHOD:

1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat and add rice, cumin, and salt. Cook, stirring constantly, until puffy and golden.

2. Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth. Bring to a boil.

3. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
SOURCE: Tequisha Erk
