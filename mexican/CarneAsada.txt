Carne Asada

4 8-10oz skirt steak or top sirloin

Marinade
2 c soy sauce
4 cloves fresh garlic
2 T fajita seasoning
2 T black pepper
1 T Lawry's seasoning
1/2 onion, chopped
3 sprigs cilantro
1 Serrano chile, cut in strips with seeds removed
1/2 c olive oil

Combine all ingredients in a containe large enough to hold the meat. Add steaks to marinade, cover and refrigerate for 1-2 hours. Remove steaks from marinade and broil or grill to preferred doneness. Discard the marinade. Serve with flour tortillas, rice, pico, etc.

From Pancho & Lefty's Restaurant cookbook.
