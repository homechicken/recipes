Pineapple Carrot Habanero Pickles

When you seek sweet tropical heat, tangy pickled pineapple slivers tamed by mild carrots with smoldering habanero flashes will light your fire!

Ingredients:

2 cups fresh pineapple chunks
1 lb. carrots, cut spears
1 habanero chili, sliced rounds
1 three-inch cinnamon stick
1 star anise pod
4 black peppercorns
4 whole cloves

Brine:
1 3⁄4 cups rice vinegar
1 1⁄4 cups water
3 tbsp organic cane sugar
2 tsp fine sea salt

Instructions:
Sterilize a 1-quart mason jar in boiling water, then pack the prepped ingredients vertically inside.

Mix the brine ingredients in a saucepan. Bring the brine just to a boil, occasionally stirring until the sugar and salt dissolve.

Carefully fill the jar with hot brine, leaving 1⁄2 inch headspace.

Apply the lid, then cool it completely.

Refrigerate the jar for the flavors to develop for a week before serving the fiery fruity pickles.
