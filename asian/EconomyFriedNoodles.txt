Economy Noodles
serves 2

4 oz rice vermicelli or whatever noodles
1 T canola oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
3 T soy sauce
2 t fish sauce
1 1/2 t sugar
1 C bean sprouts
white pepper

Prepare the noodles according to package. Drain and set aside.

Heat oil in a wok over high heat until shimmering. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Remove from heat and stir in soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Stir until dissolved.

Return to heat, add bean sprouts and stir-fry 1 minute. Add the noodles and cook, tossing them until they absorb the sauce and are heated through. Finish with a dusting of white pepper.
