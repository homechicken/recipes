Spicy Cold Celery
Serves 4

1T soy sauce
1T sesame oil
1T rice vinegar
1t sugar
1t chili oil or substitute whatever
4 large celery stalks, sliced thinly on an angle

Stir liquid ingredients together with spices in medium bowl until sugar dissolves. Add the celery and fold to coat it in the dressing. Let stand for 5 minutes and then eat. The celery will keep in the fridge, getting spicier all the while, for about 3 days.
