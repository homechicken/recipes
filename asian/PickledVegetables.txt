Basic Pickled Vegetables

8oz vegetables (cucumber, carrot, daikon, eggplant, cabbage, ginger,etc), washed, trimmed, and sliced into pieces
1 c rice wine vinegar
1 T sugar
1 t salt

Stir vinegar, sugar, and salt together until dissolved.

Add vegetables and toss until coated.

Cover and refrigerate 1-3 hours before serving.
