Pickled Napa Cabbage

1 lb napa cabbage
1 1/2 T salt
1 T toasted sesame oil
1 T toasted sesame seeds

Remove any damaged outer leaves from the cabbage. Slice into 1⁄2-inch wide pieces.

Place the cabbage in a 1-quart jar or sealable bag. Sprinkle with salt (2.5% of the cabbage weight). Mix well.

Refrigerate for at least 4 hours to lightly pickle. Rinse the cabbage, then squeeze out the moisture.

Toss the cabbage pieces with sesame oil and seeds. Enjoy chilled.
