Marinated Teriyaki Beef Skewers

INGREDIENTS:

- 3/4 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup pineapple juice
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/8 cup vegetable oil
- 1 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 2 pounds boneless top round steak, cut into 1/4 inch slices
- bamboo skewers, soaked in water


METHOD:

1. Preparation Slice the beef into 1-1/2 to 2-inch wide strips

2. Whisk brown sugar, soy sauce, pineapple juice, water, vegetable oil, and garlic together. Pour the marinade into a large ziploc bag and drop beef slices into the mixture. Marinate for 24 hours.

3. Remove beef from the marinade, shaking to remove any excess liquid. Discard marinade. Thread beef slices in a zig-zag onto the skewers.

4. Cook on a preheated grill at 325 degrees about 3 minutes per sideSOURCE: Traeger Grills
