Pickled Ginger

9 oz young ginger
1/3 cup plus 1 T white sugar
2 t salt
2/3 cup rice vinegar
2 small pieces of kombu seaweed, optional

Scrape off any brown bits of ginger, then slice it paper-thin or julienne.

Toss with 1 tbsp of sugar and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.

Pour just-boiled water over ginger to cover. Let sit for 20 seconds, then drain.

In a saucepan, bring the vinegar, 1⁄3 cup sugar, and kombu to a boil.

Remove from the heat and pour it over the ginger.

Once cool, refrigerate it for 1-3 days before serving. Keeps refrigerated for 6 months.
