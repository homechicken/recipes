Sushi Rice

For the rice:
2 c uncooked sushi rice
water
1 t dashi powder

For the vinegar:
4 T rice vinegar
2 T sugar
2 t salt

Wash rice, add proper amount of water and 1t dashi powder. Cook rice. Turn out into a large bowl.

Dissolve the sugar and salt in the rice vinegar. Use low heat if necessary. Pour over hot rice, and cut in to mix it together. Cool the rice, covered by a moistened towel so it doesn't dry out.
