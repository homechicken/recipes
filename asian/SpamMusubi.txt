Spam Musubi

Make sushi rice:
6 cups Sho Chiku Bai or other short-grained sushi rice, washed 3 times
Add 1Tbsp dashi (optional) to water, stir in
Cook with a little extra water

For the vinegar, combine:
6 T rice vinegar
1 Tbps sugar
2 t salt

Once cooled, pour vinegar mixture over rice, stir to distribute evenly.

Open 2 cans of Spam, slice the long way, and grill until it starts to darken.

Scoop rice into Spam Musubi mold, add a slice of grilled Spam. Wrap with nori, then in plastic wrap. Refrigerate.
