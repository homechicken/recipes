GINGER COOKIES

ingredients:
- 2 eggs
- 1 1/2 c. butter
- 2 c. Sucanat (dried cane juice)
- 1/2 c. molasses (dark)
- 4 1/2 c. flour
- 2 tsp. baking soda
- 1 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
- 3 Tbsp. ginger (yes, this much...Super1 bulk is my secret source for affordability)
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. cloves

method:
1.  325 degree oven.

2.  Sift with fork all dry ingredients: flour, soda, cinnamon, salt, ginger, cloves.

3.  In food processor blend eggs, sugar, molasses and butter until granules have dissolved.

4.  Scrape sugar/butter mix into dry and blend to combine.

5.  Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet lined with parchment. They may also be rolled into smooth balls by hand, making them a little larger than the size of a walnut.

6.  Bake 8 minutes for chewy cookies. (fave)
     Bake 9 minutes for soft cookies.
     Bake 11 minutes for crisp cookies.

From Melody Draves, flautist
