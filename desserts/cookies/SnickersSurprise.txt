Snickers Surprise

3 1/2 c flour
1 t baking soda
1/2 t salt

1 c butter, softened
1 c peanut butter
1 c packed brown sugar
1 c sugar

2 eggs
1 t vanilla

About 24 Mini Snickers bars


In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Whisk until combined.

In a mixer on medium-low speed combine the sugar, brown sugar, butter, and peanut butter. Add eggs and vanilla.

Add flour mixture to the mixing bowl slowly until incorporated, mix until smooth. At this point it may help to refrigerate the dough.

Shape a spoonful of dough around a mini Snickers bar and place on cookie sheet.

Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
