Oatmeal Cookies

INGREDIENTS:

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 1/2 cups unbleached white flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups instant oats


METHOD:

1. Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy; add eggs and beat thoroughly. Sift flour with soda and salt. Stir into batter mixture, add rolled oats. Drop from a teaspoon about 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets.

2. Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes or until delicately browned. SOURCE: Bob's Red Mill
