Twinkie Copycat

3 1/3 c cake flour
2 c sugar
1 T baking powder
1 t salt
1/2 t baking soda
2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
1 c buttermilk, room temperature
2 T vegetable oil
5 large egg yolks
3 large egg whites
1 T vanilla

Whisk together all dry ingredients. Add butter, buttermilk, and oil. Beat on medium until smooth. Add egg yolks and vanilla, continue beating until smooth and light. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks and fold into batter. Fill molds 2/3 with batter. Bake until golden brown, about 20 minutes.

Frosting
2 large egg whites
1/2 c sugar
1/4 t cream of tartar
2 sticks unsalted butter
1 T vanilla

Combine egg whites and sugar over double boiler, beat until thick and white and reaches 160F on an instant read thermometer. Whip in mixer until the sides of the bowl cool to room temperature. Add butter 1T at a time beating constantly on high speed until super light and thick, then add vanilla. Transfer buttercream to piping bag with injector tip. Insert into cakes and squeeze bag until sides of twinkie expand.
