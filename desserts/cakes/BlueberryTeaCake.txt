Blueberry Tea Cake

1/3 c shortening
2 c flour
2 t baking powder
1/4 t salt
1 c sugar
3/4 c milk
1 egg
1 c blueberries

Put shortening in mixer, add dry ingredients and milk, mix 2 minutes. Add egg, mix 1 more minute. Stir in blueberries

Pour into an 8x8x2 greased pan and bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
