Yummy Mix
SUMMARY:

Lorien calls this 'Addiction'

INGREDIENTS:

- 1/2 box Golden Grahams cereal
- 1/2 box Rice Chex
- 1 package coconut
- 1 package sliced almonds
- 8 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup Karo light syrup
- 1 cup sugar


METHOD:

Melt butter, syrup, and sugar in a medium pot. Bring to a boil for 3-5 minutes. Mix with everything else. SOURCE: Joyce Dillman
