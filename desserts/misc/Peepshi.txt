Peepshi

INGREDIENTS:

- 3 boxes Peeps, with different colors
- 1 box rice krispies treats, must be malleable
- 1 box fruit rollup, fruit by the foot


METHOD:

1. Slice of 3/16 inch lengthwise slices from rice krispies treats.

2. Have fun making different types of "sushi" with the colors you have available. See picture for ideas. SOURCE: SeriousEats.com
