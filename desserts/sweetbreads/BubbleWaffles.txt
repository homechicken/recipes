Hong Kong Bubble Waffles

Makes about 4 waffles

Whisk together:
1 c flour
1 1/2 t baking powder
2 t Bird's custard powder
3 T tapioca starch

Add:
2 eggs
2/3 c sugar
2 T milk
1/3 c + 3 T water

Stir in:
1 t vanilla
2 T vegetable oil

Cover and refrigerate for a day. Really, make it a day in advance. Use a squeeze bottle or pancake pen to fill the bubble waffle maker to prevent over-filling.
