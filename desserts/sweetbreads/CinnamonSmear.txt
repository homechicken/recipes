Cinnamon Smear

INGREDIENTS

NAME	WEIGHT	MEASURE
Butter, unsalted, softened	2 lb	4 cups
Sugar, brown, firmly packed	1 lb 9 oz	5 cups
Cinnamon, ground	2 oz	1/2 cup
Gold Medal™ All-Purpose Flour (14323)	14 oz	3 1/2 cups
Vanilla		1 tsp
Water, cool (approx. 72°F)	8 oz	1 cup

METHOD

Place all ingredients EXCEPT water in mixer bowl.
Mix using paddle attachment on medium speed 2 minutes. 
Add water. Mix on low speed 1 minute. Stop mixer; scrape bowl and paddle.
Mix on medium speed an additional 2-3 minutes or until smooth and creamy.
