Blueberry Muffins

INGREDIENTS:

- 2 1/4 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar, +2 tablespoons
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 1/2 cups blueberries, fresh if available
  Crumb Topping
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 cup butter, +2 tablespoons, cubed
- 2 1/4 teaspoons cinnamon


METHOD:

Preheat oven to 400. Grease muffin cups or use paper liners. Mix dry and wet muffin ingredients separate then add the two mixes together. Fold in blueberries. Fill muffin cups 3/4 of the way full and sprinkle with crumb topping. Bake 20-25 minutes.SOURCE: Gordon Ramsay
