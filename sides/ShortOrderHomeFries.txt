Short Order Home Fries

INGREDIENTS:

- 1 1/2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, cut into 1/4 inch pieces
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 onion, chopped fine
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- pepper, to taste


METHOD:

1. Arrange potatoes in large microwave-safe bowl. Top with 1 tablespoon butter and cover tightly with plastic wrap. Microwave on high until edges of potatoes begin to soften, 5-7 minutes, shaking bowl without removing plastic halfway through cooking.

2. Melt 1 tablespoon butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook until softened and golden brown, about 8 minutes. Transfer to small bowl.

3. Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in empty skillet over medium heat. Add potatoes and pack down with spatula. Cook without moving until underside of potatoes is brown, 5-7 minutes. Turn potatoes, pack down again, and continue to cook until well browned and crisp, 5-7 minutes. Reduce heat to medium low and continue cooking, stirring potatoes ever few minutes, until crusty, 9-12 minutes. Stir in onion, garlic salt, salt, and pepper.SOURCE: ATK Feed
