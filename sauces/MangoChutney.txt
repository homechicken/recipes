Mango Chutney
Note: Goes well with Alaskan Curried Shrimp

6 large mangoes
6 garlic cloves, chopped
2 c sugar
2 apples, peeled and chopped
12 oz (about 2 cups) raisins
1 T mustard seed
1 T fresh ginger finely chopped
2 c wine vinegar
3 red chilis
2 green chilis

Add everything but the mangoes to the pan. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Stir until sugar dissolves.

Peel and take the seeds out of the mangoes. Slice the mangoes. Add the sliced mangoes to the pan and cook about 30 minutes or until thick.
