Hot Fudge Sauce

INGREDIENTS:

- 4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, 60%
- 4 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
- 1 1/2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 cup light corn syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla


METHOD:

1. In a heavy saucepan, combine the chocolate, butter, sugar, water, and light corn syrup.

2. Stir the mixture continuously over medium heat until the completely melted and smooth.

3. Bring to a boil, lower the heat and continue simmering for 10 minutes.

4. Remove from the heat and stir in the vanilla extract.

5. Store covered in the refrigerator. SOURCE: Ghirardelli chocolate
