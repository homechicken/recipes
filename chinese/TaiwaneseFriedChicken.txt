Taiwanese Fried Chicken

Ingredients
1 pound chicken breast tenders cut into 1-inch chunks
1 tablespoon minced garlic (about 4-5 cloves)
1 tablespoon grated ginger (from a chunky 2-inch piece)
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons cooking sake or Chinese rice wine
1/2 cup potato starch
Vegetable oil for deep-frying
Sea salt and/or Five Spice powder for sprinkling

Instructions
Mix the chicken, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, cooking wine, and potato starch in a large bowl. Cover and marinate in the refrigeraton for 24 hours.

Pour about 2-inches of oil into a medium saucepot. Heat over medium-high heat (until 375 degrees F if you have a deep-fry thermometer). Deep-fry the chicken pieces in batches until golden brown, 3-4 minutes. Scoop out with a slotted spoon and drain on a wire rack.

Sprinkle with sea salt and Five Spice powder to taste and serve immediately.
