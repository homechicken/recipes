Scott Hensey Toy Wax Recipe

Note: Also works well as machining wax

8.46 ounces/240 grams of Carnauba
8.46 ounces/240 grams of Candelilla
2.11 ounces/60 grams of Bee wax
47.61 ounces/1350 grams of Paraffin wax
52 ounces/1500 grams of Talc
? Red, ? Brown, ? White Crayons for color: Come up with your own color!!

Melt everything together, add the talc. Stir only after everything is completely melted. Cast at 170 degrees.
