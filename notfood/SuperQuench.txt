Super Quench 
Blacksmithing liquid for quenching mild steel
DO NOT QUENCH HIGHER CARBON STEEL with this

5 gallons water
5 pounds salt
1 bottle dishwasher rinse agent (250ml)
1 bottle blue dawn 570 ml

Mix it up well, keep the lid on when not using.

To use:
Give the mixture a good stir
Get the work to full orange
Quench quickly and actively
