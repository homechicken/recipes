Ink Wash Recipe (for painting miniatures, etc)

Distilled water
Liquitex Matte Medium
Liquitex Flow Aid
Acrylic waterproof ink (Daler Rowney, etc)

30ml bottles, filler bottles, mixing containers, etc

Mix distilled water to flow aid in 10:1 ratio.

Add drops of ink to 30ml wash bottle, then fill bottle half-way with matte medium, then the rest of the way with the water/flow aid mix.

To thicken wash, use more matte medium than water/flow aid. To thin, use more water/flow aid mix.

Keep track of wash recipes in a notebook or something.

Heavy black wash: 60 drops Black ink
Light black wash: 20 drops Black
Parchment: 40 drops Flesh Tint ink
Flesh Wash: 40 drops Burnt Umber
Dark Sepia: 40 drops Sepia
Blue: 40 drops Rowney Blue

Purple: 40 drops Purple Lake
