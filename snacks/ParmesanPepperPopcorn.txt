Parmesan Pepper Popcorn

3 T vegetable oil
1/2 c popcorn kernels
2 T unsalted butter, melted
1 ounce (1/2 c) grated parmesan cheese
2 t pepper
1/4 t salt

INSTRUCTIONS
Heat oil and 3 kernels in large saucepan over medium-high heat until kernels pop. Remove pan from heat, add remaining kernels, cover, and let sit for 30 seconds.

Return pan to medium-high heat. Continue to cook with lid slightly ajar until popping slows to about 2 seconds between pops. Transfer popcorn to large bowl. Add melted butter and toss to coat popcorn. Combine Parmesan, pepper, and salt; add to popcorn; and toss to combine. Serve.
