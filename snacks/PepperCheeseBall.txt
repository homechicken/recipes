Pepper Cheese Ball

8 oz cream cheese
1/4 c butter
1/4 t savory
1/8 t parsley
1/8 t dill
1/8 t basil
1/8 t marjoram
1/8 t salt
1/8 t garlic powder

Blend cheese and butter together, then add spices and mix well. Chill mixture until you can roll it up into a ball or log, then roll it into medium-ground black pepper and serve.
